What is a View?
Your initial efforts when you write a Rogue Wave® Views program will be focused on creating and combining views where people can display and possibly interact with your program.
A view is a visual place holder—a rectangular area on your screen—where elements of your Rogue Wave Views application are displayed.
A View
Each view is distinguished by its:
location (x,y coordinates that you define),
size (height and width that you define),
visibility (a view might be present but not visible).
You create visible elements of your Rogue Wave Views application by combining views and their contents.
