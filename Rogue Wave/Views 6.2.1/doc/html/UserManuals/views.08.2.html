View Hierarchies: Two Perspectives
There are two ways of looking at how views are constructed:
Window-oriented. From the viewpoint of somebody sitting in front of a screen and using Rogue Wave Views, different kinds of views are assembled into various windows having different appearances. In fact they are created one after the other.
Class-oriented. From a C++ viewpoint, the IlvView and related classes enable you to create the different kinds of views that together create windows.
