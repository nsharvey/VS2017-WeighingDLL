Menu Bars and Toolbars
In Rogue Wave® Views Gadgets, menu bars and toolbars are implemented by the classes IlvMenuBar and IlvToolBar respectively. Both these classes derive from IlvAbstractBar, a subclass of IlvAbstractMenu.
