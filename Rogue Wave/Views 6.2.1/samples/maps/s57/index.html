Sample: s57viewer

Rogue Wave Views Maps S57 Sample

This sample shows how to load S57 data.

Data are read from the .000 file.


: Activates the tooltip interactor allowing you to show the attributes of a geometry (if any) by clicking it.
: Empties the manager.
: Loads an .000 S57 file.

Classes involved:

  • IlvS57Reader
  • IlvS57Loader
