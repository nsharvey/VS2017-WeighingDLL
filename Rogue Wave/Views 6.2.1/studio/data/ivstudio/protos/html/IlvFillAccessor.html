Fill

 The Fill accessor operates on two polygon objects contained in a prototype: a filled polygon and a filler polygon. The value handled by the accessor represents a filling level. When the level is changed, the points of the filler polygon are modified to fill the filled polygon at the specified level. An angle can be specified to fill the polygon in any direction.

Parameters

  • Filled Graphic Node: Must be an IlvPolygon graphic node

  • Filler Graphic Node: Must be an IlvPolygon graphic node

  • Angle: A float that indicates the angle at which the fill will be done.
