Sample: timescale

Show the IlvCalendarScale and IlvTimeScale Classes

An example of how to use an IlvCalendarScale that can be used in a specialized graphic object.

This sample displays a time scale. Use 'Z' and 'U' to zoom or unzoom in both directions, and 'X' and 'I' to zoom and unzoom horizontally. The IlvCalendarScale class will adapt itself to the zoom level by displaying different rows.

Classes involved:

  • IlvCalendarScale
  • IlvTimeScale
